Emerging Trend

From "Chatbot" to "Agent"

The 2023-2024 era was defined by LLMs that could talk. The 2026 era is defined by LAMs (Large Action Models) that can do. We are witnessing the automated execution of complex, multi-step workflows.

  • Autonomous Scheduling: Agents access the DMS, check shop capacity, and book appointments without human confirmation.
  • Negotiation: AI agents authorized to negotiate trade-in values within pre-set gross margin guardrails.
  • Inventory Re-pricing: Real-time price adjustments based on local competitor scraping and "Days to Turn" targets.
